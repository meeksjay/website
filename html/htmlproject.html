Actor and awards won for the portrayal of Gus

Gus was portrayed by Giancarlo Esposito

awards won for the portrayal of Gus:

  • Esposito won the Critics' Choice Television Award
    for Best Supporting Actor in a Drama Series
  • Received three nominations for the Primetime Emmy Award
    for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Drama Series

Other rankings given to the character "Gus":
  • Paste ranked Gus number 3 in its list of the 20 Best Characters of 2011
  • TV Guide named him No.3 in their 2013 list of
    The 60 Nastiest Villains of All Time
  • in 2016, Rolling Stone ranked him No.7 of their
    "40 Greatest TV Villains of All Time"
